Privacy Policy


1. Data We Collect

When you contact us, we collect necessary information such as your name, email address, phone number (if provided via WhatsApp), and project details.

2. Purpose of Collection

Your data is used strictly for communication, project requirements discussion, and delivering final design files. We do not use your data for spam.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We respect your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Form submissions are handled securely, and project files are stored safely.

Terms & Conditions


1. Payment Terms

  • A 50% non-refundable advance payment is required to commence work.
  • The remaining 50% must be paid before the final, unwatermarked files are delivered.

2. Revisions & Refunds

  • Every design project includes 2–3 rounds of revisions. Further revisions may incur extra charges.
  • No refunds will be issued once the design work has started.

3. Timelines

  • Standard delivery time is between 2 to 5 business days, depending on project complexity.

4. Client Responsibilities

  • Clients must provide clear instructions, high-resolution logos, and specific text copy before the project begins.

5. Designer Rights

  • Divine Graphics reserves the right to showcase the completed designs in our portfolio and social media channels unless a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is explicitly signed.
